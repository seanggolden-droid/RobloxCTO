# Cyber Zone Specification: Neon Metropolis

## 1. Aesthetic
End-game area. Nocturnal, high-tech, and vibrant.
- **Dimensions:** 400 x 400 studs.
- **Terrain:** Flat metal panels (Color: Dark Blue/Black). Neon light strips (0.5 studs wide) along the grid.
- **Atmosphere:** Deep purple night sky, strong neon glow (Bloom enabled).

## 2. Key Structures

### A. Digital Citadel
- **Base:** 80 x 80 studs.
- **Height:** 120 studs.
- **Material:** Glass and SmoothPlastic.
- **Design:** Geometric tower with rotating neon rings.

### B. High-Tech Incubator
- **Style:** Futuristic capsule with holographic displays.
- **Material:** Glass/Neon.

## 3. Environment Elements
- **Energy Crystal Clusters:** Digital theme (Cyan/Magenta). Square/Cuboid shapes instead of natural shards.
- **Floating Screens:** Parts with SurfaceGuis showing scrolling code or monster stats.
- **Cables:** 2-stud thick black pipes snaking across the floor.

## 4. NPC Spawn Points
- **Positions:** 10 points located on raised platforms (20x20x5 studs) scattered throughout the city.
