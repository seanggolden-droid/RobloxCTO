# Battle Arena Specification

## 1. Design & Layout
A circular, purpose-built arena designed for monster combat and wave-based survival.
- **Dimensions:** Radius 50 studs.
- **Floor:** Packed Dirt/Metal (depending on zone context).
- **Boundary:** Circular "Wave Barrier" - a semi-transparent blue forcefield (Height: 15 studs).

## 2. Combat Mechanics Build-Support
- **NPC Spawn Points:** 1 central point and 4 perimeter points (Radius 40 studs).
- **Player Position:** Center of the arena.
- **Barriers:** Entrance gates that close when a battle starts.

## 3. Visual Feedback
- **Timer Display:** A large floating BillboardGui above the center.
- **HP Bars:** Placeholder areas for UI elements above NPCs.
