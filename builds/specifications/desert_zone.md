# Desert Zone Specification: Scorched Sands

## 1. Aesthetic
Ancient and harsh. High-contrast lighting and sandy dunes.
- **Dimensions:** 400 x 400 studs.
- **Terrain:** Sand material (Color: Deep Orange). Rolling dunes (Height variation: 0-40 studs).
- **Atmosphere:** Heat haze, hazy yellow sky.

## 2. Key Structures

### A. The Great Pyramid
- **Base:** 100 x 100 studs.
- **Height:** 80 studs.
- **Material:** Sandstone.
- **Interior:** A hollow chamber with fire-themed lighting. Contains a secondary Incubator for higher-tier eggs.

### B. Sand Dunes
- **Design:** Smooth terrain curves creating natural bowls and ridges.
- **Placement:** Surrounding the Pyramid.

## 3. Environment Elements
- **Energy Crystal Clusters:** Fire-themed crystals (Orange/Red Neon).
- **Cacti:** Various Saguro-style meshes.
- **Bones:** Giant dragon ribcage mesh (30x50x20 studs) partially buried in sand.

## 4. NPC Spawn Points
- **Positions:** 8 points scattered around the dunes and the entrance to the Pyramid.
