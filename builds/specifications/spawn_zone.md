# Spawn Zone Specification: The Gateway

## 1. Aesthetic
A clean, majestic marble and gold plaza. This is the central hub where players start and return to.
- **Dimensions:** 150 x 150 studs.
- **Terrain:** Flat, Concrete/Marble material (Color: White/Light Grey).
- **Atmosphere:** Bright, clear, sense of wonder.

## 2. Key Structures

### A. The Great Incubator (Centerpiece)
- **Base:** Octagonal tiered platform (Radius: 20 studs). Material: Marble with Gold edges.
- **Top:** A massive floating glass egg (20x25x20 studs) containing golden swirling particles.
- **Interface Area:** 3 small pedestals (3x3x4 studs) surrounding the base where players interact to hatch.

### B. Portal Plaza
- **Location:** North edge of the hub.
- **Portals:** 3 large stone arches (15x20x5 studs) leading to Forest, Desert, and Cyber zones.
- **Visuals:** Each portal has a semi-transparent effect matching its destination (Green, Orange, Blue).

### C. Shop NPC Stalls
- **Structure:** 2 stylized wooden/stone stalls (15x10x12 studs).
- **NPC Positions:** Centered behind the counters (1x1x5 stud area).
- **Decor:** Banners hanging from the stalls indicating "Monsters" and "Boosts".

## 3. Placement & Navigation
- **Spawn Pad:** A central 10x10 stud glowing pad.
- **Pathways:** 15-stud wide paths leading from the center to Portals and Shops.
- **Boundary:** High marble walls with gold trim (Height: 30 studs) to prevent players from falling off.
