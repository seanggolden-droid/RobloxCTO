# Monetization & Premium Builds: Monster Mash Simulator

## 1. VIP Lounge: The Royal Roost
An exclusive area for VIP gamepass holders. Designed to look opulent and high-status.
- **Location:** West side of the Spawn Zone hub.
- **Dimensions:** 40 x 40 x 25 studs.
- **Aesthetic:** Royal Purple and Gold.
- **Materials:** Marble (White), Neon (Gold), SmoothPlastic (Purple).
- **Features:**
    - `Gated Entrance`: A semi-transparent purple barrier (10x15x1 studs) that checks for VIP status.
    - `VIP Incubator`: A smaller, more ornate version of the central incubator with a 1.2x luck multiplier visual (golden particles).
    - `Seating Area`: Low-poly plush sofas and a golden fountain.
    - `Exclusives Display`: Holographic pedestals showing off the "VIP Slime".

## 2. Premium Shop: The Gilded Vault
A specialized shop for developer products and exclusive Robux-only eggs.
- **Location:** East side of the Spawn Zone hub.
- **Dimensions:** 25 x 25 x 20 studs.
- **Aesthetic:** High-tech Gold.
- **Materials:** Metal (Gold), Glass (Light Blue tint), Neon (Cyan).
- **Features:**
    - `Vault Door`: A large, non-functional but impressive circular gear door on the back wall.
    - `Product Displays`: 3 floating glass pods (5x5x5 studs) showcasing rotating items.
    - `Holographic NPC`: A translucent cyan NPC stand for the "Merchant of Wonders".

## 3. Ad Billboard Placements
Standardized frames for Roblox Image/Video ads.
- **Dimensions:** 24 x 12 x 2 studs (Standard 2:1 aspect ratio).
- **Material:** Metal (Black) frame with a Neon (White) backboard.
- **Placements:**
    - `Main Hub`: 2 boards facing the spawn pad.
    - `Portal Plaza`: 1 board above each portal archway.
    - `Battle Arenas`: 1 board on the perimeter fence.

## 4. Progression Gate Visual Polish
Enhancements to the standard zone barriers to make unlocking feel rewarding.
- **Base Gate:** 50x100x10 studs semi-transparent neon.
- **Unlock Animation Specs:**
    - `Particle Burst`: When unlocked, trigger a massive burst of "Essence" colored particles (matched to the zone).
    - `Sound Placeholder`: Note for engineer to play a "Chime" sound.
    - `Transparency Fade`: Gate should fade from 0.5 to 1 over 1.5 seconds.
    - `BillboardGui Update`: Display "UNLOCKED!" in glowing green text before the gate disappears.
