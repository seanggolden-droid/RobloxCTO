# Forest Zone Specification: Verdant Wilds

## 1. Aesthetic
Classic starter zone. Natural, hilly terrain with dense (but traversable) foliage.
- **Dimensions:** 400 x 400 studs.
- **Terrain:** Grass material (Color: Bright Green). Hilly elevation (0-20 studs).
- **Atmosphere:** Morning sun, gentle breeze particles.

## 2. Environment Elements

### A. Energy Crystal Clusters
- **Distribution:** Scattered randomly but denser near the center and perimeter.
- **Crystal Design:** (See energy_crystals.md for details) - Forest crystals are Green.

### B. Natural Assets
- **Trees:** Low-poly stylized oak trees (10x15x10 studs).
- **Rocks:** Smooth grey boulder meshes scattered for cover.
- **Water:** A small pond (Radius: 30 studs) at the center with a waterfall from a small cliff.

### C. NPC Spawn Points
- **Positions:** 5 marked areas (5x5 studs) near the pond where NPC mobs spawn.

## 3. Navigation
- **Entrance:** Connected to Spawn Zone via the Green Portal.
- **Exit:** A progression gate at the far end (requires 5k Essence) leading to the Desert Zone.
