# VIP Lounge Specification: The Royal Roost

## 1. Aesthetic
An exclusive high-status area for VIP pass holders. Designed with opulence in mind.
- **Location:** West side of the Spawn Zone hub.
- **Dimensions:** 40 x 40 studs.
- **Materials:** White Marble, Gold (Neon), Purple (SmoothPlastic).
- **Atmosphere:** Exclusive, high-status glow.

## 2. Key Features

### A. Entrance Barrier
- **Dimensions:** 15 x 15 x 1 studs.
- **Material:** Semi-transparent neon (Color: Royal Purple, Transparency: 0.7).
- **Function:** A teleporter or barrier that checks for VIP status.
- **Visuals:** Floating "VIP ONLY" text using BillboardGui.

### B. 3x Energy Crystal (Exclusive)
- **Design:** A massive, multi-faceted crystal (8x15x8 studs).
- **Color:** Golden Neon with deep purple core particles.
- **Collection Multiplier:** 3x Energy compared to standard crystals.
- **Regen Rate:** Fast (re-spawns every 10 seconds).

### C. Lounge Furniture
- **Sofas:** Low-poly plush purple sofas with gold trim (10x4x4 studs).
- **Fountain:** A central golden fountain (Radius 5, Height 6).
- **VIP Incubator:** An ornate gold and glass incubator (8x8x10 studs) with premium hatching animations.

## 3. Placement Specs
- **Elevation:** Raised 5 studs above the main plaza floor.
- **Lighting:** Localized point lights (Gold) to create a warm, premium feel.
