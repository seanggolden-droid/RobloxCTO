# Ad Billboard Frames Specification

## 1. Structure Design
Standardized frames for displaying in-game advertisements or ImageLabels.
- **Dimensions:** 15 x 10 x 2 studs.
- **Aspect Ratio:** 1.5:1.
- **Materials:** Metal (Dark Grey) frame, Neon (White) backboard for the ad surface.

## 2. Placements
- **Spawn Hub:** 2 frames mounted on the marble walls facing the spawn pad.
- **Forest -> Desert Path:** 1 frame placed on a rock outcrop at the halfway point.
- **Cyber Zone Entrance:** 1 frame mounted on a high-tech pillar (10x10x20 studs).

## 3. Integration
- **Lighting:** Small neon spotlights (White) at the top corners of each frame.
- **SurfaceGui:** Frame part includes a SurfaceGui named "AdSurface" for the engineering team to map textures.
