# Premium Shop Specification: The Gilded Vault

## 1. Aesthetic
A larger, more impressive shop specifically for Robux-only purchases.
- **Dimensions:** 30 x 25 x 20 studs.
- **Materials:** Glass (Light Blue tint), Gold (Metal), Gold (Neon).
- **Aesthetic:** High-tech, high-status vault.

## 2. Structure Features

### A. Exterior
- **Walls:** Massive glass panels with gold metal framing.
- **Entrance:** Automatic sliding glass doors (8x12 studs).
- **Signage:** "GILDED VAULT" in neon gold 3D text above the entrance.

### B. Interior
- **Shelving:** 3 tiers of floating glass shelves (Length 20, Depth 3) along the back wall.
- **Display Pods:** 3 central glass pods (5x5x8 studs) for "Featured Items".
- **Merchant Counter:** A solid marble counter with golden neon accents (Length 15, Height 4).

## 3. Placement Specs
- **Location:** East side of the Spawn Zone hub, mirrored to the VIP Lounge.
- **Lighting:** Intense Bloom effect on neon parts; bright internal point lights.
